Our Services

StudySwap offers a variety of services to help students succeed. Our platform provides a convenient and affordable way to:

Buy Tools

Find textbooks, calculators, lab equipment, and more at great prices from other students.

Sell Tools

Sell your used textbooks and other tools to other students and earn some extra cash.

Exchange Tools

Swap your old tools with other students for the ones you need, saving money and resources.

Donate Tools

Give back to the community by donating your used tools to students in need.

Rent Tools

Rent expensive tools like lab equipment or specialized software for short-term use.
